Hi! My name is Christina Rubino and I am a print designer, painter and musician from Brooklyn, NY. I attended St. Edmund prepatory High School in Brooklyn and went on to further my education in illustration and fint art by attending the School of Visual Arts in Manhattan. I majored in illustration, but have always had a passion for technology and design. Let me tell you, using a pencil or an Adobe program is much easier than designing with CSS! I am trying to navigate the learning curve by applying the basics I have learned at the NYCDA school and making this website.

Well, hopefully I don't screw it up terribly, and the nice teacher wont have to jumble his brain too much to figure out what the hell is going on in those CSS stylesheets attached.
